What is this?

The World Values Survey is an organization which surveys people from different countries on their personal values and attitudes.

This is a visualization regarding values and attitudes towards jobs and working life. Survey respondants were asked to name the aspects of a job that perceivably are the most important.

The parallel coordinate system above displays the percentage of respondees from each surveyed country who mentioned each aspect as an important one!

Controls

Brush: Drag vertically along an axis.
Remove Brush: Tap the axis background.
Reorder Axes: Drag a label horizontally.
Invert Axis: Tap an axis label.
Remove Axis: Drag axis label to the left edge.
